Foresee issues early to safeguard yields and boost productivity

Understanding crop diseases is crucial for maintaining healthy crops and ensuring food security. Crop diseases can significantly impact crop yield and quality, which in turn affects farmers' income and the availability of food. Early detection and accurate diagnosis of these diseases can help in taking timely and effective measures to manage and mitigate their effects.
